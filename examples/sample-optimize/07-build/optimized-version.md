# 優化後的最終版本 / Optimized version

> Deterministic de-AI pass (1 filler phrase(s) stripped) + a structure to finish. The creative rewrite — the part that needs taste — is the operator's or the host LLM's; the agents do not invent facts.

## Cleaned draft

our world-class KYC onboarding flow will seamlessly leverage cutting-edge technology to improve the customer experience and unlock tremendous value. We collect source of funds for enhanced due diligence under ASIC rules in Australia. It is robust, best-in-class, and a true game-changer.

## Structure to lift it to the top 1%
1. **Open with the single-minded proposition** — the one promise, in one line, with a number.
2. **Lead each claim with its evidence** — every benefit gets a measured figure beside it (the Data officer flagged the gaps in Step 2).
3. **Cut the filler** — remove: `in conclusion`, `leverage`, `seamlessly`, `robust`, `cutting-edge`, `game-changer`, `unlock`, `world-class`.
4. **Name the reader** — speak to the exact buyer / job-to-be-done, not "users" in general.
5. **Make it findable** — one head term, repeated with intent; scannable headings.
