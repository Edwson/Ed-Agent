## Checkpoint · Frame the problem

> ⚠ Intent is UNSTATED. The agent can only infer the goal from the requirement text — which is the #1 source of "technically right, business-wrong" outcomes. State the business goal, the business definition of done, and the non-goals.

_Inferred from the requirement text only: "In conclusion, our world-class KYC onboarding will seamlessly leverage cutting-edge technology to improve the experience". This is a guess, not the stated business intent._

## Open questions
1. What is the business GOAL? (the outcome it must produce — not the task)
2. What is DONE in business terms? (the signal that it actually worked)
3. What are the NON-GOALS / out of scope? (so a local optimum cannot dig a global pit)
