# 06 · Creative brief (Creative Director)

- **Objective:** Launch a landing page for a new savings product with a signup funnel
- **Audience:** the job-to-be-done behind the requirement (segment by job, not demographics alone)
- **Single-minded proposition:** one promise the page must land
- **Tone:** confident, specific, claim-substantiated
- **Hero message:** answer what / for whom / why-now in the first viewport
- **Channels (hypothesis):** test ≥2, measure, double down on the winner

> Creative judgment stays human — the Director proposes; the operator approves at the gate.
